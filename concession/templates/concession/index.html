What is Onesession ?

Onesession is an app that allows you to fill and post your railway concession without hassle.


How do I add concession form using Onesession ?

First, create a new form by filling out the form on this page.

Once an form is created you will be able to upload your concession form.


What are some Advantages of Onesession over physical concession queues ?

  • We don't have to go to that annoying guy

  • Real time verification

  • Don't have to stand in queues for long amount of time

  • Students can access and fill concession forms from anywhere and anytime from any device
